Our Passion

Why Everlasting Flowers?

Traditional grocery store flowers wither and fade in a matter of days. Our hand-crafted floral grocery items are crafted stem-by-stem using premium combed cotton yarns, fine structural double-crepe papers, and ethically sourced biological wool felts.

We treat our crafts with the high standard of organic farm-to-table grocers. Our flowers never fade, need zero water, never trigger allergies, and remain a gorgeous testament of affection for years to come.
